About Me

I was born in Virginia and lived there until I was 33. I graduated in 2008 from Virginia Tech with a BS in Wildlife Science. My final year of college I injured my back, which made a future of frollicing in the woods pretty difficult, so I started teaching myself graphic design. I've been designing logos, t-shirts, flyers, and the like since. After dabbling a little in web design, I decided to expand my skillset and attend CWRU's Coding Bootcamp.

Here I am. Excited and nervous. But each day I understand coding a little bit better, and that makes me more confident....And that's a good feeling.
