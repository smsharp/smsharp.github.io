About Sara

Sara Sharp is a full stack web application and software developer with a passion for integrating code and art to make the best user experience possible.

A Wildlife Science graduate from Virginia Tech and a love for the outdoors, Sara found herself home bound after a back injury her senior year of college. With determination not to let her injury keep her down, she began teaching herself design programs so she could make t-shirts and flyers for student organizations. If she couldn't join them, she was still going to make a difference.

Since then, she has continued her graphics journey, and in 2017, attended Case Western Reverse University's Coding Bootcamp to expand her skill set. She thrives in software and application development, excited to learn new languages and constantly looking for ways to write better code. With an eye for detail, she enjoys taking the user experience to new levels.

As a freelance graphic designer for many years, she works well to communicate and understand the needs of others. She likes tackling complex problems and looking for ways to improve her skills. She looks very forward to finding a career in a fun, fast-paced environment, where she can use and refine her coding skills and mix in her creative eye.
